Clean Community

One app, less trash. Start making a difference with Clean Community!

Our app incentivizes trash collection, connects like-minded individuals, and simplifies finding disposal sites and supplies. Earn rewards, compete with friends, and track progress to make a lasting impact towards cleaner neighborhoods!

Give and receive supplies at our convenient supply outposts using the 4 digit code found in the app.

Locate your nearest trash and recycling cans easily using our interactive maps.

Plan litter pickup events with friends and others in your area to connect and maximize your efforts.

Test the live prototype!
